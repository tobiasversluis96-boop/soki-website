Our answer

This is why we built SOKI.

Not a silent, anonymous spa. Not a luxury wellness club. Something much more human: a living, breathing venue where you can sweat, relax and also discover art, talks, DJ sets or small performances.

Without loud music and alcohol, but with warmth, atmosphere, soft sounds and real encounters. A place you can also visit on a Friday night with friends, or on your own, to unwind, connect and recharge.

SOKI is a hidden oasis just beyond the city limits of Utrecht, built by Luke & Tobias — two friends who wanted to turn the sauna into a new form of socialising.

What we stand for

More than a sauna

🌿

Wellness without walls

Sauna sessions and cold plunges that are affordable and accessible to everyone in Utrecht. Not just a luxury, but a shared experience.

🎵

Culture & community

A living, breathing venue, not a silent spa. Expect art, talks, DJ sets and small performances woven into the sauna experience.

🤝

Our sessions

Four ways to experience SOKI

Every session includes access to sauna, ice baths and our lounge. Complimentary water and maté tea included.

Everyday Sauna

Free-flow access to our sauna and ice baths. Move at your own pace, unwind in the lounge.

€15  ·  50 min

Social Sauna

Extended sauna and ice bath access plus unlimited lounge time. Curl up, catch up, or meet someone new.

€20  ·  80 min

Ambient Sauna

A curated DJ transforms the sauna into an immersive listening lounge. Stay for the full 4-hour event with live music.

€25  ·  70 min + 4hr event

Aufguss / Opgieting

Traditional ritual led by our sauna masters. Essential oils, guided visualisation and a homemade salt scrub.

€25  ·  90 min
