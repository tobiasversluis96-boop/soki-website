our answer

this is why we built SOKI.

Not a silent, anonymous spa. Not a luxury wellness club. Something more human: a place where you sweat, relax and can also catch a DJ set, a talk or a small performance.

Without loud music and alcohol, but with warmth, soft sounds and real encounters. Come on a Friday night with friends, or on your own. Either works.

SOKI is a spot tucked away just outside Utrecht city centre, built by Luke & Tobias, two friends who wanted to make the sauna a real place to socialise.

what we stand for

more than a sauna

Wellness without walls

Sauna sessions and cold plunges that are affordable and accessible to everyone in Utrecht. Not just a luxury, but a shared experience.

Culture & community

A living, breathing venue, not a silent spa. Expect art, talks, DJ sets and small performances woven into the sauna experience.

Real human connection

A place to meet people without the noise of a bar. Come alone or with friends. Either works.
