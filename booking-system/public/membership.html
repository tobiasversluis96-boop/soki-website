FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do credits work?
With the Everyday Member plan you get 4 credits per month. An Everyday Sauna session costs 1 credit, while Social Sauna, Ambient Sauna and Aufguss sessions each cost 2 credits. When booking, credits are automatically deducted instead of paying with card.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account page. Your membership stays active until the end of your current billing period — you won't be charged again after cancelling.
When do credits reset?
Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle (the date you subscribed). Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.
What if I run out of credits?
If you don't have enough credits for a session, you can still book and pay with card as usual. Or consider upgrading to the Unlimited plan for worry-free access.
