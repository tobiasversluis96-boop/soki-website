Good to know

Before you come

🧖

What to bring

Swimwear (mandatory), two towels, flip-flops or slides, and a water bottle. We provide complimentary water and maté tea.

🏠

Facilities

Traditional Finnish sauna, ice bath tubs, relaxation areas, rain showers, changing facilities and an outdoor terrace.

📍

Getting here

Europalaan 2B, 3526 KS Utrecht (just outside Vechtclub XL). Easily accessible by bike or bus. Free parking available on site.

🕐

Opening hours

Wed – Thu: 16:00–21:00
Fri: 16:00–23:00
Sat: 10:00–23:00
Sun: 10:00–20:00
Mon – Tue: Closed

🎟️

Booking & cancellation

Online booking recommended. Cancel up to 48 hours ahead for full credit. 24–48 hours: 50% refund. Under 24 hours: no refund.

👥

Groups & solo visits

Solo visits are very welcome. Groups of up to 15 people per time slot. For larger bookings, reach out to us directly.
