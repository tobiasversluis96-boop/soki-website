A hidden oasis just beyond the city limits

Reshaping the sauna experience

Step inside and leave the noise behind. This is a space to slow down, reconnect with yourself, meet others and break free from everyday constraints.

Instead of a silent, anonymous spa, we're building a living, breathing venue where you can sweat, relax and also discover art, talks, DJ sets or small performances.

Our aim is to offer a true alternative to the usual social spaces like bars and clubs: a warm, inspiring place where wellbeing, creativity and genuine human connection come first.
